  • About Me

    Angelica Blanch is a designer based in Vancouver, raised in the Philippines and Indonesia. Having moved around the world, her designs are often inspired by celebrating culture, and tackling issues that matter.

    When not designing, you can either find her curled up reading her next TBR book, or searching for new word games to play (her current favourite is Strands by NYT)
