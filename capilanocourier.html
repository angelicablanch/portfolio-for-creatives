Capilano Courier

Art Direction | Layout Design | Print & Web Production

Capilano Courier is Capilano University's student-run newspaper. Courier publishes monthly issues from September to April.

As the Art Director, I design the layouts for the monthly issue and zines, and produce print and web ready files for them. I also enhance the art direction and branding to curate them for each year's new demands.

I also help produce illustrations wherever needed. I collaborate closely with the crew members, like the Production Manager and Editor-in-Chief, to further keep the visual branding strong across all platforms.
