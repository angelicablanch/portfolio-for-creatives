Lola:
Filipino
Restaurant

Branding | Layout Design | Copywriting | Packaging | Website

Lola, grandmother in Tagalog, features a community dining experience, echoing the cultural tradition of Kamayan.

Based in Vancouver, dishes are served on a banana leaf atop a long table, embodying the feeling of home through food, people, and culture.

Inspired by my grandmother, the brand aims to pass down traditions for generations to come, and reconnect the Filipino-Canadian community back to their culturally-rich and unique motherland.
