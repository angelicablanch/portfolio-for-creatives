Rooted:
Employment
Program

Branding | Art Direction | Layout Design | Merchandise

With having produce grown in urban lots, Rooted helps employ and support at-risk and disadvantaged young adults by setting them up for their career prospects through gardening and sales operation. By learning important life and employment skills, Rooted gives these young adults an opportunity that may not have been possible for them in the first place.

Group project with Annika Yi and Phoebe Verdon.
