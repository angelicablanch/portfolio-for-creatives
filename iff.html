Inclusive Film Finder

Art Direction | Layout Design | Research

Filmmakers frequently face challenges/barriers in implementing authentic cultural representation in their creations. The Inclusive Film Finder (IFF), a quarterly magazine and website, helps movie-goers and filmmakers shift how they view, enjoy, and rate movies. IFF enables film fans to see and rate films based on how culturally representative they are, and provide valuable feedback to filmmakers. Featured articles by film journalists review movies, both current offerings and Hollywood favourites, through a critical race lens.

Group project with Caleb Tsai, Kyle Rask, and Tin Raganit
My roles: Team Lead, Research, Art Direction, Layout Design
